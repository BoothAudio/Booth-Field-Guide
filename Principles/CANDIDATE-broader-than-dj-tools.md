# Principle: Booth Is Broader Than DJ Tooling

Status: Candidate
Owner: Nick approval required for established status

## Claim

Booth should be framed as helping people make better audio decisions, with DJs
as the first target because their workflows expose useful, actionable source
information.

## Evidence

- Historical memory records that Booth Audio is being built to help people make
  better audio decisions.
- Historical memory records that DJs are the first target because their
  workflows expose useful, actionable source information, and Booth should not
  be framed as only DJ tooling long term.

## Scope

Applies to brand positioning, roadmap framing, landing-page copy, product scope,
and customer development.

## Limits

This does not prevent focused DJ-specific execution in the current product. It
prevents narrow positioning from becoming the long-term company identity by
accident.

## Contradictory Evidence

None recorded yet.
