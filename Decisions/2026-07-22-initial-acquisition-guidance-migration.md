# Decision: Preserve Initial Acquisition Guidance From Operational Memory

Status: Draft
Date: 2026-07-22
Deciders: Nick review needed

## Context

Before the Booth Field Guide became canonical, several Booth acquisition
guidance points lived in operational memory. During Field Guide integration,
those points were removed from `MEMORY.md` so Booth philosophy would not be
duplicated there.

This draft preserves the prior guidance for founder review without promoting it
into established philosophy.

## Historical Guidance Captured

- Booth should recommend and explain the best trusted acquisition path rather
  than present a pile of outbound links.
- Booth's trusted acquisition network has included official artist sites,
  official label sites, Bandcamp, Beatport, trusted DJ stores, official
  SoundCloud downloads, and verified download gates.
- Acquisition UI should separate source verifiability from match confidence.
- A paid or trusted store can be easy to verify even when Booth has not
  confirmed the exact version.
- A key product metric has been whether a DJ can confidently answer: "This is
  the best place to acquire this track."

## Established Guidance

- `../Constitution.md`: Trust is earned, never claimed.
- `../Constitution.md`: Confidence never exceeds evidence.
- `../Constitution.md`: Remove effort, not understanding.

## Decision

No new established principle is created by this migration.

The acquisition guidance above should be treated as historical product reasoning
that appears to apply the existing constitutional principles to source-finding
and acquisition workflows.
